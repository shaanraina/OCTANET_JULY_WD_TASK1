Our Products

Straight Bourbon Whiskey

Elegant and exceptionally rich, complex & balanced taste with a touch of sweetness & spice. A four-grain, high-rye straight bourbon whiskey with a mash bill of 65% corn, 22% rye, wheat and barley making up the rest. The copper column and copper pot-doubler distillation method creates a spirit full of flavor and texture. Then the spirit is aged for a minimum of 4 years in virgin American oak barrels with a deep char #4 on the staves and # 2 char on the barrel heads.

American Blended Rye Whiskey

Big and bold, aromatic & full of flavor. A remarkable four-grain blended rye whiskey with a final mash bill of 77% Rye, 16% corn, with wheat and barley making up the rest. Made in small batches, this rye recipe combines our signature bourbon blended with a distinctive straight rye mash bill, both aged in new, deep-charred oak barrels for a minimum of four years. The result is a well-integrated and complex rye whiskey full of robust flavors with a full-bodied mouthfeel and extra-long finish.
